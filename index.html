🔥 MOGGING PROMPT TEMPLATE
FORMAT INSTRUCTIONS: Write an AI video prompt for a short (~8 second) video clip. The style should be exaggerated, absurd, and cinematic with a chaotic vlog or meme-like tone, similar to YouTube or TikTok shorts. Follow this structure: Opening Tag (Bold): Briefly describe the type of video (e.g., "YouTube vlog-style jungle adventure" or "cinematic war zone TikTok meme"). Keep it punchy. Visual Description (Bold Title): Use vivid, dynamic imagery. Describe the setting, the main character(s), what they're wearing, especially if they have bold, reflective Pit Viper ski goggle-style sunglasses, and how the camera is moving (GoPro-style, handheld, zoom, shaky, dramatic, etc). Keep it weird, funny, or over-the-top. Dialogue (Italic or Bold + Quotation): Include a direct quote from the main character(s). Make the line hilarious, surreal, meme-worthy, or dramatic — always in-character and fitting the absurdity of the moment. Keep the tone playful, confident, or unhinged. Ending (Optional): Describe how the scene ends — a zoom-in, cut to black, a character's reaction, a freeze-frame, etc. Tone: Think: viral, chaotic, cinematic, meme-laced, satire of modern content, sometimes historical or futuristic, always wearing Pit Viper ski goggles for max mog energy.
